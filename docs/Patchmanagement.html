Identify Vulnerabilities

In this step, vulnerabilities in the system are identified using various vulnerability scanning tools, such as Nessus or OpenVAS, to ensure that no security loopholes remain.

→
priority_high

Prioritize Patches

Here, patches are prioritized based on the severity of vulnerabilities discovered. Critical patches are applied first to protect the system from high-risk threats.

→
assessment

Test Patches

Before applying patches to the live environment, they are tested in a controlled environment to ensure compatibility and avoid disruptions.

→
system_update_alt

Deploy Patches

Once tested, patches are deployed across the production environment, ensuring minimal downtime and keeping the system secure.

→
check_circle

Verify & Validate

After deployment, patches are verified to ensure they were successfully applied. Systems are checked for any anomalies to confirm security measures are intact.

→
description

Document & Report

All applied patches are documented for compliance and auditing purposes. Reports are created to track patching activities and review the system's security status.
