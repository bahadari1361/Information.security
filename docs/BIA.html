Business Impact Analysis (BIA) in ISO 27001

Business Impact Analysis (BIA) is a crucial part of the ISO 27001 standard. It involves identifying the potential impacts of disruptions to business operations and determining recovery priorities. The diagram below illustrates the BIA process and its components:

business

Identify Critical Assets

Identify the key business assets, systems, and processes essential for operations.

assessment

Assess Impact

Analyze the potential consequences of disruptions on these critical assets.

→
timer

Determine Recovery Time Objective (RTO)

Define the maximum acceptable downtime for each critical asset.

→
restore

Determine Recovery Point Objective (RPO)

Establish the acceptable data loss for each critical asset.

→
layers

Identify Dependencies

Identify the interdependencies between critical assets, processes, and systems.

→
build

Develop Mitigation Strategies

Formulate strategies to minimize the impact of disruptions and ensure recovery.
