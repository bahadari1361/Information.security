Incident Response Manager

The Incident Response Manager oversees the entire incident response process, coordinating the team and ensuring effective communication and decision-making during incidents.

Incident Handler

Incident Handlers are responsible for leading the response to security incidents, identifying the scope, managing containment, and collaborating with other teams for resolution.

Forensic Expert

The Forensic Expert analyzes evidence related to incidents, identifies the cause and extent of the breach, and supports investigations with digital evidence.

Malware Analyst

Malware Analysts examine malicious code, identifying how it spreads, its impact, and how to defend against future attacks. They often collaborate with forensic experts.

Security Analyst

Security Analysts evaluate the network and systems for vulnerabilities, analyzing threats and incidents to protect against future breaches and enhance security posture.

Communications Specialist

Responsible for internal and external communication, managing the flow of information between teams, stakeholders, and the public, including media handling and crisis communication.

Legal Advisor

The Legal Advisor ensures that incident response actions comply with laws and regulations, especially in terms of data breaches, privacy laws, and regulatory requirements.

Recovery Specialist

Recovery Specialists lead the process of restoring systems and data, ensuring continuity of services and minimizing downtime after an incident.

IT Support

IT Support is responsible for ensuring technical systems are operational, assisting with remediation efforts, and helping to restore systems back to normal after an incident.
