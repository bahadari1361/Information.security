What is Business Continuity Planning (BCP)?

Business Continuity Planning (BCP) is a critical aspect of ISO 27001 that ensures an organization can continue its operations during and after a disruptive event. The BCP process involves identifying potential risks, establishing recovery strategies, and ensuring that the necessary resources are in place to minimize downtime and maintain essential operations.

Key Elements of BCP

  • Risk Assessment and Business Impact Analysis (BIA)
  • Recovery Strategies
  • Plan Development and Testing
  • Training and Awareness
  • Regular Review and Updates
