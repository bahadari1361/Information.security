Business Continuity Planning (BCP)

Risk Assessment

Identify potential threats to the business and evaluate risks associated with business operations.

Business Impact Analysis

Analyze the critical functions of the business and the impact of disruptions on them.

Strategy Development

Develop strategies to ensure business continuity, including disaster recovery and emergency plans.

Plan Implementation

Implement the BCP strategies and ensure resources, tools, and personnel are ready for deployment.

Testing & Drills

Test the BCP through simulations and drills to ensure readiness in case of actual disruption.

Plan Maintenance

Regularly update and maintain the BCP to address new risks and ensure it remains effective.
