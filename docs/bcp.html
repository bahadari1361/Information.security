What is Business Continuity Planning (BCP)?

Business Continuity Planning (BCP) is a crucial part of the ISO 27001 standard. It ensures that an organization can continue operating during and after a disruptive event. The key components of BCP involve risk management, recovery strategies, and continuous improvement.

Key Elements of BCP

  • Risk Assessment and Business Impact Analysis (BIA)
  • Recovery Strategies
  • Plan Development and Testing
  • Training and Awareness
  • Regular Review and Updates
