ISO/IEC 27001 Finalized Audit Plan

Audit Plan Checklist

Task Description Status
Define Audit Scope Identify the boundaries, systems, departments, and processes to be audited.
Set Audit Objectives Establish compliance goals, effectiveness assessments, and improvement opportunities.
Identify Key Stakeholders List involved personnel, including management, IT, and compliance teams.
Develop Audit Schedule Prepare a timeline for activities, including document reviews, interviews, and reporting.
Allocate Audit Resources Assign roles and responsibilities to the audit team and prepare required tools.
Prepare Pre-Audit Notifications Inform relevant stakeholders about the audit schedule and their responsibilities.
Conduct Pre-Audit Meetings Discuss scope, objectives, and methodologies with stakeholders.

Audit Checklists and Tools

Tool/Checklist Purpose Status
Information Security Policy Review Checklist Evaluate the completeness and effectiveness of the organization's security policies.
Risk Assessment Checklist Verify that all identified risks have been assessed and treated appropriately.
Statement of Applicability (SoA) Ensure the SoA aligns with the organization's risk treatment plan and implemented controls.
Annex A Control Checklist Confirm implementation of controls in areas such as access management, cryptography, and physical security.
Document Review Checklist Check that all required documents are up-to-date and accessible.
Incident Response Plan Ensure procedures for detecting, reporting, and addressing security incidents are effective.
Audit Reporting Tool Prepare templates for findings, observations, and recommendations.
