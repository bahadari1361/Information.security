Key Sections:

Governance and Risk Management

  • Develop and maintain information security policies.
  • Establish organizational structures for information security.
  • Perform regular risk assessments and treatment.

Endpoint Devices

  • Include desktop computers, laptops, and mobile devices.
  • Integrate Internet of Things (IoT) devices and servers.

Security Controls

  • Implement antivirus, endpoint detection, and device encryption.
  • Use multi-factor authentication and role-based access controls.
  • Enforce network segmentation and intrusion detection systems.

Supporting Processes

  • Deploy centralized logging and SIEM systems for monitoring.
  • Establish incident response plans with actionable playbooks.
  • Automate patch management and conduct vulnerability scans.

Training and Awareness

  • Provide security awareness programs to all employees.
  • Conduct regular phishing simulations and feedback sessions.

Compliance Monitoring

  • Perform regular internal and external audits.
  • Ensure continuous improvement using the Plan-Do-Check-Act cycle.
