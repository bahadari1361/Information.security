IT Audits with ISO 27001

Pre-Audit Planning

Define the audit scope, objectives, and criteria. Identify key stakeholders and gather necessary resources.

Initial Assessment

Evaluate the organization's existing ISMS (Information Security Management System) for compliance with ISO 27001 standards.

Audit Execution

Perform the audit by reviewing policies, procedures, controls, and conducting interviews to assess compliance.

Audit Reporting

Document audit findings, non-compliances, and recommendations for improvements.

Corrective Actions

Work with stakeholders to implement corrective actions and improve the ISMS based on audit results.

Post-Audit Follow-up

Ensure that corrective actions are implemented and evaluate their effectiveness to ensure compliance is maintained.

IT Audit Team

Lead Auditor

Responsible for overseeing the entire audit process, ensuring compliance with ISO 27001, and leading the audit team.

Security Expert

Specializes in identifying security gaps and weaknesses in the organization's ISMS and IT infrastructure.

Documentation Specialist

Focuses on gathering, reviewing, and organizing audit documentation, including policies and procedures.

Risk Analyst

Assesses potential risks and vulnerabilities within the organization's systems and operations.

Communication Specialist

Coordinates communication between audit team members and organizational stakeholders, ensuring transparency and clarity.
