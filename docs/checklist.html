A.5. Information Security Policies Ensure policies are established, approved, and communicated.
A.6. Organization of Information Security Define roles, responsibilities, and contact with authorities or groups.
A.7. Human Resource Security Implement security during hiring, training, and termination processes.
A.8. Asset Management Identify, classify, and protect organizational assets.
A.9. Access Control Ensure appropriate access controls, including user access management and authentication.
A.10. Cryptography Define and implement cryptographic controls to protect information.
A.11. Physical and Environmental Security Implement controls to secure physical premises and equipment.
A.12. Operations Security Ensure secure operations, including logging, monitoring, and malware protection.
A.13. Communications Security Secure network communications and protect data in transit.
A.14. System Acquisition, Development, and Maintenance Ensure security is integrated into the development and acquisition processes.
A.15. Supplier Relationships Manage information security risks in supplier relationships.
A.16. Information Security Incident Management Establish procedures for reporting and responding to incidents.
A.17. Business Continuity Management Ensure information security continuity during disruptions.
A.18. Compliance Ensure compliance with legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements.
Note: This checklist is a structured guide to ensure compliance with ISO/IEC 27001 standards. Customize it based on your organization's specific context and requirements.
