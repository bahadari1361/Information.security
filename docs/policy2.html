Risk Identification

Identifying potential risks that could affect the bankâ€™s operations, infrastructure, and data.

In this phase, we focused on identifying all possible risks to the bank's systems and data, including cyber threats, physical risks, operational disruptions, and regulatory risks.

  • Cyber threats: Malware, ransomware, phishing, insider threats.
  • Physical risks: Theft, natural disasters, fire.
  • Operational risks: System failures, human error, external disruptions.
  • Regulatory risks: Non-compliance with financial and data protection regulations.

We conducted regular threat modeling sessions with stakeholders to ensure a comprehensive understanding of potential risks.

Risk Evaluation

Evaluating identified risks based on their impact and likelihood to prioritize mitigation strategies.

We evaluated risks by assessing their potential impact on operations and their likelihood of occurring, ensuring that the most critical risks were addressed first.

  • Impact assessment: Severity of disruption caused by the risk.
  • Likelihood assessment: Probability of the risk occurring.
  • Risk Matrix: Categorizing risks as high, medium, or low priority based on impact and likelihood.

This process helped us focus on the highest-priority risks and allocate resources accordingly.

Risk Treatment

Implementing strategies to mitigate or eliminate identified risks through control measures.

For each identified risk, we developed a treatment plan that included strategies for mitigation or avoidance. Key actions included:

  • Firewalls and encryption: Protecting systems from unauthorized access and ensuring data security.
  • Access controls: Enforcing least-privilege access and multi-factor authentication to secure sensitive data.
  • Disaster recovery planning: Developing plans to ensure business continuity in case of disruptions.
  • Employee training: Raising awareness through regular security awareness programs.

We also worked closely with third-party vendors to ensure they followed the same security standards.
