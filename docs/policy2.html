Information Security Policies

Central Bank of Afghanistan - ISO/IEC 27001 Project

Information Security Policy

Defines the framework for developing, implementing, and reviewing security controls.

The policy establishes the organization's commitment to information security, outlining security objectives and control frameworks.

ISO 27001 Reference: Clauses 5.1, 5.2

Asset Management Policy

Defines how assets should be classified, managed, and protected.

The policy outlines asset ownership, classification, protection, and disposal procedures.

ISO 27001 Reference: Clause 8.1

Access Control Policy

Ensures the appropriate use of access privileges.

The policy enforces the principle of least privilege, user authentication, and role-based access rights.

ISO 27001 Reference: Clause 9.1.2, A.9.1.1 to A.9.2.6

Data Protection and Privacy Policy

Manages personal data in compliance with privacy regulations.

This policy governs the collection, processing, and retention of personal data, ensuring compliance with privacy laws.

ISO 27001 Reference: Clauses 7.5, 8.2

Cryptographic Controls Policy

Ensures data confidentiality and integrity through encryption.

The policy defines encryption standards, key management, and acceptable cryptographic algorithms.

ISO 27001 Reference: Clause 10.1.1, A.10.1

Incident Management and Response Policy

Defines procedures for identifying and responding to incidents.

The policy outlines procedures for incident reporting, categorization, escalation, and investigation.

ISO 27001 Reference: Clause 16.1, A.16.1.1

Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery Policy

Ensures continuity of critical operations during incidents.

The policy covers business impact analysis, recovery objectives, backup procedures, and testing strategies.

ISO 27001 Reference: Clause 17, A.17.1

Physical and Environmental Security Policy

Prevents unauthorized access and protects equipment.

The policy defines physical security measures to protect premises, data centers, and equipment, including environmental controls.

ISO 27001 Reference: Clause 11.1, A.11.1

Human Resource Security Policy

Ensures employees understand their responsibilities.

The policy covers onboarding, training, and security awareness programs.

ISO 27001 Reference: Clause 7.2, A.7.1.1, A.7.2.1
