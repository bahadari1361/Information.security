Compliance with ISO/IEC 27001

This section outlines how the encryption project adhered to ISO/IEC 27001 standards, ensuring that the encryption strategy was in full compliance with the required Information Security Management System (ISMS) controls.

Compliance Highlights:

  • Encryption policies aligned with ISO/IEC 27001 A.10 (Cryptographic Controls).
  • Data protection measures integrated into the ISMS framework.
  • Continuous audits to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements.

The project helped the Central Bank of Afghanistan achieve a high level of compliance with international data security standards, ensuring that financial data remained secure from unauthorized access or breaches.
