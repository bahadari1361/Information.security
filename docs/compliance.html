Compliance with ISO/IEC 27001

The encryption project adhered to strict standards to ensure full compliance with ISO/IEC 27001, a key international standard for information security management systems (ISMS). This ensured that the bank's encryption efforts were both effective and legally compliant.

Compliance Steps:

  • Reviewing ISO/IEC 27001 requirements for encryption and data protection
  • Implementing appropriate encryption measures in line with the standard
  • Conducting regular audits to assess compliance
  • Documenting all processes and results for transparency and future audits
