Portfolio: Information Security Management

Central Bank of Afghanistan

Overview

This portfolio showcases my work as the Information Security Manager at the Central Bank of Afghanistan. It includes policies, plans, audits, assessments, and tools used to safeguard the bank's critical infrastructure. My role involved ensuring the security of sensitive data, networks, and IT systems. Additionally, I contributed to implementing ISO 27001 standards to strengthen the bank's security framework.
