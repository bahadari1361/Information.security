Portfolio Project: Information Security Management at the Central Bank of Afghanistan

Overview

As the Information Security Manager at the Central Bank of Afghanistan, I was responsible for designing, implementing, and managing a comprehensive set of security measures to protect the bank’s sensitive data, networks, and IT infrastructure. This portfolio outlines my work in developing security policies, business continuity plans, incident response strategies, conducting IT audits, penetration testing, and security assessments to ensure the organization’s resilience against evolving cyber threats. Additionally, I played a key role in the deployment of ISO 27001 to align the bank's security framework with international best practices.

1. Information Security Policy Development

  • Network Security Policy: Designed guidelines for the protection of the bank's network infrastructure.
  • Email Security Policy: Established protocols for secure email communication.
  • User Management and Password Policy: Developed a comprehensive password policy.
  • Data Disposal Policy: Defined secure methods for data disposal.
  • Internet Usage Policy: Instituted controls to prevent unauthorized data uploads.
  • FTP Server Policy: Implemented strict access controls for FTP servers.
  • Patch Management Policy: Developed a patch management protocol.
  • Remote Desktop Policy: Defined secure access protocols for remote desktop connections.

2. Business Continuity Planning (BCP) Development

I led the creation of the Business Continuity Plan (BCP), ensuring the bank could maintain operations during and after disruptive events. Key components included risk assessments, disaster recovery strategies, and crisis management procedures.

Conclusion

As the Information Security Manager at the Central Bank of Afghanistan, I played a pivotal role in designing and implementing security strategies to protect the organization’s IT systems, data, and operations. From developing robust security policies to hands-on tasks like penetration testing and incident response planning, my work ensured that the bank remained resilient against evolving cyber threats.
