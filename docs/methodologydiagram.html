Network Route Circular Chart - Methodology Phases

Document Review
Stakeholder Interviews
Vulnerability Scanning
Penetration Testing
Document Review: Review critical documents like security policies, risk management frameworks, and business continuity plans.
Stakeholder Interviews: Conduct interviews with security personnel to identify concerns and gather insights.
Vulnerability Scanning: Use tools like Nessus and Nmap to perform vulnerability assessments.
Penetration Testing: Simulate attacks to evaluate the effectiveness of existing security measures.
