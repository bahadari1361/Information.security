Business Continuity Planning (BCP)

Risk Assessment

Identify potential threats to the business and evaluate risks associated with business operations.

Business Impact Analysis

Analyze the critical functions of the business and the impact of disruptions on them.

Strategy Development

Develop strategies to ensure business continuity, including disaster recovery and emergency plans.

Plan Implementation

Implement the BCP strategies and ensure resources, tools, and personnel are ready for deployment.

Testing & Drills

Test the BCP through simulations and drills to ensure readiness in case of actual disruption.

Plan Maintenance

Regularly update and maintain the BCP to address new risks and ensure it remains effective.

BCP Team

BCP Manager

Oversees the BCP process, ensuring all activities align with business goals and risks are mitigated.

Risk Management

Identifies and evaluates potential risks to the organization, helping to shape the BCP strategies.

IT/Technology

Ensures IT infrastructure and systems are protected and can be restored during disruptions.

Business Unit Representatives

Provide insights into critical functions and help develop recovery strategies for each department.

HR & Communication

Ensures employee well-being and communication, making sure everyone is informed during a crisis.
