Design and Implementation

This section details the design and implementation of the encryption protocols for the Central Bank of Afghanistan. The encryption strategy was focused on securing sensitive data in transit and at rest, using industry-standard algorithms and best practices to ensure confidentiality, integrity, and availability of the bank’s financial data.

Key Highlights:

  • Implementation of AES-256 for data encryption at rest.
  • Use of RSA and SSL/TLS for secure communications.
  • Deployment of Full Disk Encryption (FDE) on critical servers.

The encryption protocols were designed to ensure compliance with ISO/IEC 27001, safeguarding the bank’s operations and customer data against unauthorized access and cyber threats.
