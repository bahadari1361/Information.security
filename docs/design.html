Design and Implementation

The encryption project at the Central Bank of Afghanistan involved the design and implementation of robust encryption protocols to secure sensitive financial data. This project was critical in ensuring that all data transmitted and stored within the bank's systems was protected against unauthorized access.

Key Steps:

  • Selection of strong encryption algorithms (AES-256, RSA, SSL/TLS)
  • Development of an encryption architecture for both data at rest and in transit
  • Integration of encryption protocols into existing IT infrastructure
  • Testing and validation to ensure seamless implementation
