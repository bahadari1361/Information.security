Central Bank of Afghanistan Project

Monitoring and Logging Overview

Comprehensive monitoring and logging strategies to detect, analyze, and respond to security incidents.

The Monitoring & Logging project was implemented to ensure continuous surveillance of the bank's IT infrastructure. It included the design, implementation, and management of monitoring systems that tracked network traffic, system health, and security events to enable proactive detection and response to potential security incidents.

  • Centralized Logging: Implementation of a centralized logging system to aggregate logs from various systems, servers, and network devices.
  • Real-time Monitoring: 24/7 monitoring to detect unusual activities and provide quick responses to potential threats.
  • Incident Detection: Setup of automated alerting systems to notify security teams of potential incidents like unauthorized access or data breaches.
  • Compliance Tracking: Ensuring the logging practices comply with industry regulations like GDPR and ISO/IEC 27001.

The project ensured that the bank had full visibility over its network and security posture, helping prevent and detect threats in real-time.

Incident Response and Investigation

Key activities in detecting and responding to security incidents, supported by detailed logs and alerts.

Our monitoring system was integrated with the incident response framework to ensure immediate investigation and resolution of any detected anomalies. This involved:

  • Alerting Mechanism: Automated alerts generated by the logging system were sent to the Security Operations Center (SOC) for investigation.
  • Log Correlation: Correlating logs from different sources to identify attack patterns or security breaches.
  • Forensic Investigation: Conducting forensic analysis to understand the scope of any detected incidents.
  • Incident Documentation: Maintaining thorough documentation for every incident, including actions taken and lessons learned.

This allowed the team to quickly respond to potential security threats, reducing risk and minimizing the impact on operations.

Reporting and Compliance

Generating detailed reports for internal and external audits to ensure compliance with regulations and industry standards.

The project involved the creation of reports for both internal stakeholders and external auditors. This reporting system provided detailed insights into the health of the bank’s IT infrastructure and security measures. Activities included:

  • Audit Trails: Ensuring logs are maintained securely and are available for auditing at any time.
  • Compliance Reports: Generating reports to demonstrate adherence to industry standards like ISO/IEC 27001, GDPR, and other regulatory frameworks.
  • Regular Reviews: Ensuring the logs were reviewed periodically to identify vulnerabilities and risks.
  • Evidence of Risk Mitigation: Reports detailing how security incidents were managed and mitigated.

These reports were essential for maintaining the trust of regulators and customers while demonstrating the bank's commitment to robust security practices.

Data Retention and Privacy

Ensuring secure storage and retention of logs in compliance with data protection laws.

Logs and monitoring data were retained according to data retention policies and industry best practices. Key aspects included:

  • Secure Storage: Logs were securely stored in an encrypted format, ensuring confidentiality and integrity.
  • Retention Policy: Logs were retained for a defined period in compliance with regulatory and operational requirements.
  • Data Privacy: Ensuring that personally identifiable information (PII) was anonymized or excluded from logs to meet privacy requirements.
  • Log Purging: Regular purging of outdated logs to ensure compliance with the bank’s data retention policies.

These measures ensured that the bank maintained high standards of data privacy while retaining logs necessary for investigation and compliance purposes.
