Team Leader

Coordinates all response activities, ensures alignment with ISO 27001, and acts as the primary decision-maker during incidents.

Forensic Analyst

Conducts investigations, gathers evidence, and performs detailed analyses to identify root causes and methods of attack.

IT Administrator

Handles system and network configurations, isolates affected systems, and implements technical fixes.

Legal Consultant

Advises on legal obligations, ensures compliance, and communicates with external legal entities if needed.

Communications Officer

Manages internal and external communication, including updates to stakeholders and the public.
