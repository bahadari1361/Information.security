Incident Response Plan (IRP) Portfolio

The Incident Response Plan (IRP) Portfolio Project demonstrates my approach to handling cybersecurity incidents, outlining the methodology and steps involved in developing, executing, and refining a comprehensive plan. The objective is to ensure that organizations are prepared to effectively detect, respond, and recover from security incidents while minimizing business disruption.

This project serves as a showcase of my ability to establish effective incident response frameworks, incorporating proactive measures, response protocols, and recovery strategies to safeguard business continuity.

The objective of this portfolio is to display my capability in creating and executing an Incident Response Plan. This plan includes several key components:

  • Structured incident response phases
  • Detection, containment, and eradication strategies
  • Recovery and post-incident analysis
  • Testing and refining the plan through simulations and drills

By showcasing this plan, I aim to illustrate my expertise in leading incident response efforts and improving organizational resilience against cybersecurity threats.
