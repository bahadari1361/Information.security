Incident Response Plan (IRP)

Identification

Detect and identify potential security incidents or breaches in systems or networks.

Containment

Contain the incident to prevent further damage or impact to systems or data.

Eradication

Remove the cause of the incident, such as malware, and prevent further occurrence.

Recovery

Restore affected systems and data, and ensure systems are back to normal operation.

Lessons Learned

Review and document the incident, determine lessons learned, and adjust policies and procedures.

IRP Team

Incident Response Manager

Leads the incident response process and ensures the team follows the established protocols.

Security Analyst

Analyzes incidents, investigates the scope, and works to mitigate the impact.

IT Specialist

Supports the containment and eradication of incidents through technical means.

Business Continuity Planner

Ensures recovery processes are in place and works with business units to maintain operations.

Communication Lead

Manages internal and external communication during the incident response process.
