Professional Assessment Steps

Initial Assessment

Understand the organization's security posture and define the scope.

Risk Assessment

Identify and evaluate potential risks to determine their impact.

Vulnerability Assessment

Conduct scans to find vulnerabilities in the system or application.

Penetration Testing

Attempt to exploit the identified vulnerabilities to gauge their risk.

Security Controls

Implement measures to mitigate vulnerabilities and secure the system.

Compliance Review

Ensure all security controls comply with industry standards and regulations.
