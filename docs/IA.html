Audit Process Details

  • Audit Planning: Define the scope, objectives, and timelines for the audit.
  • Pre-Audit Documentation Review: Review of existing documentation, policies, and records.
  • Fieldwork/On-Site Audit: Conduct interviews, document reviews, and observe practices on-site.
  • Risk Assessment & Control Evaluation: Evaluate risk management and control effectiveness.
  • Non-Conformity Reporting: Identify any non-conformities and report them.
  • Audit Closing Meeting: Discuss findings and corrective actions with the management team.
  • Audit Report: Prepare a detailed report documenting findings, corrective actions, and recommendations.
  • Follow-Up Audit: Re-assess the corrective actions taken after the initial audit.
