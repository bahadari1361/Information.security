IT Audits with ISO 27001

Audit Planning

Define the audit scope, objectives, and criteria. Identify key stakeholders and gather necessary resources.

Initial Assessment

Evaluate the organization's existing ISMS (Information Security Management System) for compliance with ISO 27001 standards.

Audit Execution

Perform the audit by reviewing policies, procedures, controls, and conducting interviews to assess compliance.

Audit Reporting

Document audit findings, non-compliances, and recommendations for improvements.

Corrective Actions

Work with stakeholders to implement corrective actions and improve the ISMS based on audit results.

Post-Audit Follow-up

Ensure that corrective actions are implemented and evaluate their effectiveness to ensure compliance is maintained.
