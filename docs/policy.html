ISO 27001
Information Security Policy

1. Purpose and Scope

The purpose of this policy is to establish a robust framework for managing information security risks within the organization. It applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who interact with organizational data.

2. Roles and Responsibilities

Designates clear responsibilities for information security within the organization. The Information Security Manager (ISM) oversees the policy's implementation, while employees adhere to security protocols and report incidents.

3. Risk Management Approach

Focuses on identifying, assessing, and mitigating information security risks through regular risk assessments, ensuring that the organization minimizes the impact of potential security breaches.

4. Data Protection Measures (DP Measures)

Defines the measures required to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of sensitive data, including encryption, access control, and data masking practices.

5. Training and Awareness

Highlights the importance of ongoing security training for all personnel to ensure they are aware of current threats and best practices to mitigate risks.

6. Document Control

Establishes policies for managing information security documents to ensure that all policies, procedures, and controls are up to date and easily accessible.

7. Information Security Objectives

Defines clear objectives for information security, ensuring they align with the organizationâ€™s overall goals. These objectives focus on protecting assets and ensuring legal compliance.

8. Commitment to Conformance

The organization commits to conforming to the ISO 27001 standards, ensuring continuous improvement in the Information Security Management System (ISMS).

9. IS Controls and Framework

Details the security controls based on the ISO/IEC 27001 framework. It includes physical, administrative, and technical controls to safeguard information assets.

10. Incident Management

Outlines the procedures for detecting, reporting, and responding to security incidents, ensuring swift mitigation of any information security breaches.

11. Internal Audit and Review

Describes the process of internal audits to review compliance with the ISMS and identify areas of improvement, ensuring continuous security compliance.

12. Consequences of Nonconformance

Defines the consequences of failing to comply with the information security policy, including disciplinary actions and potential legal consequences for violations.
