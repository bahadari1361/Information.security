Cybersecurity Project Portfolio

Hashmatullah Bahadari | Cybersecurity Specialist | ISO/IEC 27001 Lead Auditor

Purpose and Scope

This project outlines the **Information Security Management System (ISMS)** designed to secure systems, networks, and data assets. The scope includes penetration testing, risk management, and IT audits for critical infrastructure.

Information Security Objectives

Ensured the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data through the implementation of **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)**, secure network design, and rigorous **penetration testing** using tools like Kali Linux and Nessus.

Roles and Responsibilities

As an **Information Security Manager**, I was responsible for policy writing, system hardening, IT auditing, penetration testing, and guiding IT teams to improve security measures and meet compliance standards.

Commitment to Conformance

Implemented ISO/IEC 27001 standards and **BCP** (Business Continuity Plans) to ensure business operations were secure and compliant with international regulatory requirements.

Risk Management Approach

Conducted regular **risk assessments** to identify vulnerabilities, assess potential threats, and recommend mitigation strategies to prevent unauthorized access or disruptions.

Information Security Controls

Applied **access control policies**, cryptographic tools, and system security protocols to strengthen organizational cybersecurity posture. Tools included **Nmap**, **Wireshark**, and **OWASP methodologies**.

Data Protection Measures

Developed a **Data Protection Framework** compliant with GDPR regulations, ensuring the privacy of customer and employee data through encryption, anonymization, and access control measures.

Incident Management

Created and deployed an **Incident Response Plan (IRP)** for detecting, responding to, and reporting security incidents. Utilized monitoring tools like **ntopng** for real-time network traffic analysis.

Training and Awareness

Led regular **security awareness training** for employees and stakeholders to ensure compliance with policies and mitigate risks caused by human errors or negligence.

Internal Audit and Review

Performed **IT audits** to evaluate the effectiveness of controls, ensuring ongoing conformance with ISO/IEC 27001. Continuous improvement plans were implemented based on findings.

Document Control

Maintained proper **document control** for policies, plans, and audit reports to ensure relevance and timely updates as the organization evolved and technologies advanced.

Consequences of Nonconformance

Ensured that failure to adhere to the information security policy would result in disciplinary measures, emphasizing the critical role of compliance in securing organizational assets.
