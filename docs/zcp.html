Business Continuity Planning (BCP) Portfolio

As an accomplished Information Security Manager with extensive expertise in Business Continuity Planning (BCP), I specialize in ensuring organizational resilience against disruptions. My proficiency spans policy formulation, risk assessment, recovery strategy design, and incident management, ensuring critical business functions remain operational under adverse conditions. This portfolio highlights my structured approach to BCP, illustrating my ability to safeguard organizational integrity and uphold stakeholder trust.

Policy Framework Creation: Designed comprehensive BCP frameworks tailored to organizational objectives, ensuring compliance with industry standards and regulatory requirements (e.g., ISO 22301).

Leadership Engagement: Collaborated with executive leadership to secure buy-in for BCP initiatives and established a governance model assigning clear accountability to cross-functional teams.

Threat Identification: Conducted risk assessments to identify potential disruptions, including cyberattacks, natural disasters, and operational failures.

Critical Asset Analysis: Evaluated business functions and assets to determine their criticality and interdependencies.

Recovery Objectives: Defined Recovery Time Objectives (RTOs) and Recovery Point Objectives (RPOs) for critical systems.
