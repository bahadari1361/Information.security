Business Continuity Planning (BCP) Policy and Procedures

Purpose and Scope: Clearly define the objectives and scope of the BCP, outlining the critical functions, assets, and stakeholders it encompasses.

Leadership Commitment: Highlight the commitment from leadership in fostering a resilient culture and supporting continuity initiatives.

Governance and Structure: Detail the governance structure, including the roles and responsibilities of the Business Continuity Manager and other key personnel.

Risk Identification: Assess potential risks, such as natural disasters, cyberattacks, and supply chain disruptions, that could impact operations.

Business Impact Analysis: Identify and prioritize critical functions, evaluating the operational, financial, and reputational impact of disruptions.

Strategic Goals: Outline recovery strategies and define key objectives to ensure rapid and effective restoration of services.

Internal Communication: Develop protocols for efficient communication among internal stakeholders during a crisis.

External Communication: Establish guidelines for communicating with external parties, including clients, suppliers, and regulatory bodies.
