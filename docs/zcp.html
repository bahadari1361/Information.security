Business Continuity Planning Portfolio

As an accomplished Information Security Manager with extensive expertise in Business Continuity Planning (BCP), I specialize in ensuring organizational resilience against disruptions. My proficiency spans policy formulation, risk assessment, recovery strategy design, and incident management, ensuring critical business functions remain operational under adverse conditions. This portfolio highlights my structured approach to BCP, illustrating my ability to safeguard organizational integrity and uphold stakeholder trust.

  • Policy Framework Creation: Designed comprehensive BCP frameworks tailored to organizational objectives, ensuring compliance with industry standards and regulatory requirements (e.g., ISO 22301).
  • Leadership Engagement: Collaborated with executive leadership to secure buy-in for BCP initiatives.
  • Documentation: Authored detailed policies outlining objectives, scope, and implementation protocols for BCP.
  • Threat Identification: Conducted risk assessments to identify potential disruptions, including cyberattacks, natural disasters, and operational failures.
  • Critical Asset Analysis: Evaluated business functions and assets to determine their criticality and interdependencies.
  • Impact Assessment: Performed BIAs to quantify the financial, operational, and reputational impact of potential disruptions.
  • Recovery Objectives: Defined Recovery Time Objectives (RTOs) and Recovery Point Objectives (RPOs) for critical systems.
  • Strategic Planning: Developed and implemented recovery strategies such as failover systems, disaster recovery sites, and cloud backups.
  • Process Optimization: Ensured alignment of recovery plans with organizational priorities and operational workflows.
  • Incident Identification: Established protocols for detecting and reporting incidents promptly.
  • Response Procedures: Designed response frameworks to contain, mitigate, and recover from disruptions efficiently.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Implemented monitoring tools to ensure real-time tracking of critical operations.
  • Employee Training: Designed and conducted regular training sessions for employees, emphasizing their roles in executing BCP protocols.
  • Simulation Exercises: Orchestrated tabletop exercises and live drills to validate the efficacy of continuity plans.
  • Awareness Campaigns: Fostered a culture of preparedness through targeted communication initiatives.
  • Plan Validation: Conducted regular testing of BCP components to ensure they remain effective and up-to-date.
  • Feedback Integration: Incorporated lessons learned from drills and real incidents to enhance BCP strategies.
  • Continuous Improvement: Reviewed and updated BCP documentation in response to organizational changes, technological advancements, and evolving threats.
