Central Bank of Afghanistan Project

Risk Identification

As part of the risk identification phase at the Central Bank of Afghanistan, we conducted thorough assessments of potential risks to critical banking systems. Key activities included:

  • Identification of cyber threats such as malware, ransomware, and data breaches.
  • Assessment of physical risks like theft, vandalism, and natural disasters affecting data centers.
  • Evaluation of operational risks, such as system failures, human errors, and misconfigurations.
  • Examination of regulatory risks concerning the compliance of the bank’s operations with national and international standards.

Regular threat modeling sessions were conducted to proactively identify and address potential vulnerabilities in both technical and operational processes.

Risk Evaluation

After identifying the risks, we evaluated their potential impact and likelihood. This step ensured a structured approach to mitigating risks that could harm the bank’s operations:

  • Impact: Determining the severity of disruption if a particular risk occurred, especially for critical banking systems.
  • Likelihood: Assessing how likely it was for each risk to materialize based on historical data and threat intelligence.

A risk matrix was created to classify risks as high, medium, or low based on their impact and likelihood, ensuring prioritized mitigation strategies.

Risk Treatment and Mitigation

To treat identified risks, we implemented a variety of strategies aimed at reducing, transferring, or accepting those risks. Key initiatives included:

  • Deployment of advanced firewalls, encryption, and access control measures to protect sensitive financial data.
  • Design and testing of Disaster Recovery Plans to ensure business continuity in the event of a crisis.
  • Regular employee training and awareness programs to minimize human errors and ensure secure practices.
  • Ensuring compliance through third-party risk management for vendors, contractors, and partners.

Risk Monitoring and Review

Continuous monitoring and review were essential to ensure the effectiveness of risk treatment efforts. The following activities were carried out:

  • Regular audits and risk reviews to track risk levels and address emerging threats.
  • Monitoring of incidents and establishing feedback loops to adapt to changing risks.
  • Updating the bank’s overall risk posture based on new threats, vulnerabilities, and security developments.
